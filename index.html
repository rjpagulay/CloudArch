When we started our business in 2015, the pasta dough was made daily using our family recipe. Soft & silky. It was rolled through a hand crank pasta machine that was clamped onto the kitchen counter. One side of the machine slowly thins the dough to the perfect sheet consistency. The other cuts it into flat wide ribbons, a shape called Tagliatelle.

About us

We’re the mother-daughter duo of The Pasta Shoppe in Thunder Bay, ON, Canada. We believe that pasta dinners should be fresh, easy and enjoyed more frequently than the occasional Sunday. It should bring you around the table on a weekday and start a conversation before you rush off dance/hockey practice or after a long day away from home.
